likums.

L2: (B→(C→D))→((B→C)→(B→D))

L4: B∧C→C
L5: B→(C→B∧C)
L6: B→B∨C
L7: C→B∨C
L8: (B→D)→((C→D)→(B∨C→D))
L9: (B→C)→((B→¬C)→¬B)
L10: ¬B→(B→C)
L11: B∨¬B
